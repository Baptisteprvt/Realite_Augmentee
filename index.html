How to play ?

Welcome to the Bone Anatomy Challenge!

Your goal is to correctly identify bones in the 3D model to increase your score.

When you start the game, a bone will be highlighted, and multiple-choice buttons will appear on the wall, on the left of the skeleton, each labeled with different bone names.

Click on the button that you believe correctly identifies the highlighted bone.

If your answer is correct, the bone will flash green, and you'll earn points.

If you are incorrect, the bone will shatter, and you can try again with the next one.

Use the mouse to explore the model.

Complete all bones to finish the game, and strive for a perfect score!

Have fun learning and testing your knowledge of human anatomy!
